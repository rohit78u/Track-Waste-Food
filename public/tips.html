Food Waste Reduction Tips

Meal Planning

Plan your meals for the week before shopping to avoid buying unnecessary items.

  • Create a weekly meal plan
  • Check your pantry before shopping
  • Make a precise shopping list

Proper Storage

Store food correctly to maximize freshness and shelf life.

  • Use airtight containers for dry goods
  • Store produce in proper humidity
  • Freeze leftovers you won't eat soon

Creative Recipes

Transform leftovers and aging ingredients into delicious meals.

  • Make soups from vegetable scraps
  • Use overripe fruit in smoothies or baking
  • Create "clean out the fridge" stir-fries

Composting

Turn food scraps into nutrient-rich compost for your garden.

  • Set up a compost bin in your yard
  • Use a countertop bin for collection
  • Balance greens and browns in your compost

Date Management

Understand and manage food expiration dates properly.

  • Learn the difference between "use by" and "best before"
  • Organize your pantry/fridge by expiration date
  • Trust your senses - many foods last beyond dates

Portion Control

Serve appropriate portions to reduce plate waste.

  • Use smaller plates to control portions
  • Measure ingredients when cooking
  • Start with smaller servings - you can always get more
